Ramla

Ramla got hit by life which is one of the adages, Life happens. Because of this, she had to move to the village to live with grandma and her cousin, Sasha. She definitely needs to fight to get the scholarship which was her last chance of getting the best out of life, not just for her but also for her parents. It was difficult but she succeeded at the end. Ramla didn't just came to Gboka village to get pitied, what she wants is a good life and that is what she got.
